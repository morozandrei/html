  • Guitar History

    To know what we talk about here you should understand some terms.

    Guitar
    Guitar is fretted musical instrument that typically has six strings.
    Electric Guitar
    Is a guitar that has an electronic unit that gives a possibility to be connected to an amplifier in order to be heard at typical performance volumes.
    Acoustic Guitar
    Is a guitar where the vibrations from strings are transmitted from the bridge, resonating throughout the top of the guitar.
